I.Pad Thai

Description

Pad-Thai is a stir-fry dish best made wih rice noodles.It is commonly served as street food in Thailand. Pad-thai is typically made with rice noodles,shrimp,peanut,among other ingredients.The dish is usually fried in a wok or a deep frying pan.The dish has gained popularity as it's easy to prepare and the ingredients are inexpensive. That said, Let's get right into it!

ingredients

(serves 4)
  • 8 oz thick rice noodles
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves,chopped
  • 2 fresh red chiles,seeded and chopped
  • 6 oz pork tenderloin,thinly sliced
  • 4 oz shrimp,shelled,deveined and chopped
  • 8 fresh Chinese chives,snipped
  • 2 tbsp Thai fish sauce
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2tbsp jaggery or light brown sugar
  • 2 eggs,beaten
  • 3 oz fresh bean sprouts
  • 4tbsp fresh cilantro,chopped plus extra springs to garnish
  • 3/4 cup chopped unsalted peanuts

steps

  1. Prepare the noodles according to the package directions.Drain well and set aside.
  2. Heat a wok over medium-high heat,then add the oil.
  3. Stir-fry the garlic,chiles, and pork for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the shrimp and stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add the chives and noodles, then cover and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add the fish sauce,lime juice,sugar and eggs.
  7. Cook, stirring and tossing constantly to mix in the eggs.
  8. Stir in the bean sprouts,cilantro and peanuts and mix well,then transfer to serving dishes.
  9. Scatter over some extra peanuts and serve immediately, garnished with coriander springs.
