III.Hot Sesame Beef

Description

This super easy recipe comprises of beef tenderloin that is stir fried with bell peppers and broccoli.Lets dive right to it!

ingredients

(serves 4)
  • 1 oz beef tenderloin, cut into thin strips
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1/2 cup beef stock
  • 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped finely
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp chile flakes
  • 3 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 large head broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 orange bell pepper,seeded and thinly sliced
  • 1 fresh red chile,seeded and thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp chilli oil,to taste
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro, to garnish.

steps

  1. In a small bowl mix the beef with 1 tbsp of the sesame seeds.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the stock,soy sauce,ginger,garlic,cornstarch, and chile flakes.
  3. Heat a wok over medium-high heat,then add a tablespoon of the sesame oil.
  4. Stir-fry the beef for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Remove and set aside
  6. Discard any oil left in the wok,then wipe with paper towels to remove stray sesame seeds.
  7. Heat the remaining oil in the wok,add the broccoli,orange bell pepper,chile,and chilli oil, and stir fry for 23 minutes.
  8. stir in the stock mixture,cover,and let simmer for 2 minutes.
  9. Return the beef to the wok and let simmer until the juices thicken,stirring occasionally.
  10. Cook for another 1-2 minutes,Sprinkle with the remaining sesame seeds.
  11. Serve garnished with chopped cilantro.
