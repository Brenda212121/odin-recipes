II. Stir fried broccoli

Description

The broccoli is a vegetable that belongs to the cabbage family.It is a healthy food rich in minerals and vitamins with multiple culinary uses.In this classic stir-fry recipe, we're going to make use of the broccoli heads(florets).

ingredients

(serves 4)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 medium heads broccoli,cut into florets
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp superfine sugar
  • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1 garlic clove,crushed
  • pinch of hot chile flakes
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame seeds, to garnish

steps

  1. Heat a wok over a medium-high heat,then add the oil.
  2. Stir-fry the broccoli for 4-5 minutes.
  3. In a small bowl,combine soy sauce,cornstarch,sugar,ginger,garlic, and chile flakes.
  4. Add the mixture to the broccoli.
  5. Cook over gentle heat, stirring constantly,for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Transfer to a serving dish,garnish with sesame seeds and serve immediately.
